Beats Me (DJ Hero Prototype Game)

This is a small LÖVE game inspired by the DJ Hero games, built by port and Evar678 ❤. It's fully playable at the moment, but could do with more complete tracks, Settings & Help menus that aren't entirely empty, a proper name, and more effects. Right now the game looks really plain. If you have any experience with visual effects in LÖVE or at all, hit us up!
